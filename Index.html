Welcome to Ellamorph

First and formost, any and all content on this page is subject to change at anytime. Nothing is final. This page is essentially a scratchpad for my ideas. 'Ellamorph' is a pun of the main character's name, Ella, and EleMorph. Though we might go with the spelling 'Elemorph'. The prior is better in my opinion though... Or even better 'Ellamental'.

Ellamorph is a 2D puzzle platformer inspired by the likes of Ori and the Blind Forest, and A Rose in the Twilight. The player will be tasked with solving puzzles to pass through different levels. Along the way, you will find 'Element Crystals' that will allow the character to morph into the crystals respective element, unlocking new powers that will aid you in puzzle solving.
