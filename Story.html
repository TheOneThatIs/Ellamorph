Story

Our protagonist, Ella, is playing in the backyard when she spots an odd light in the forest outside her house. She makes the (poor) decision to follow it. She then gets lost. Unable to find her way back home, she wanders the woods in the gathering darkness. Eventually she finds a stange glowing crystal. She touches it and gains her first elemantal power. More stuff happens as the story progresses.
